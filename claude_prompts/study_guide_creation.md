# Study Guide Creation Prompts for O Level and A Level Students

## Objective
This document contains various prompts and guidelines for creating effective study guides tailored for O Level and A Level students. 

## 1. Purpose of Study Guides
- Why is a study guide important?
- How can it aid in better understanding of subjects?
- Discuss the role of organization in effective study preparation.

## 2. Concept Explanation Prompts
- Explain a recent topic covered in class. What are the key points?
- Provide an overview of the syllabus and highlight the most critical areas.
- Summarize a complex concept in simple terms and provide relatable examples.

## 3. Study Techniques
- Suggest specific study strategies for subjects typically covered at O Level and A Level.
- Discuss the effectiveness of group study versus solo study.
- Create a timetable template for preparing for examinations.

## 4. Revision Tips
- What are the best practices for revision?
- How should students prioritize topics based on their strengths and weaknesses?
- Include sample questions or quizzes for self-assessment.

## 5. Additional Resources
- List useful websites, books, and videos that can aid in study.
- Provide links to past papers and other exam preparation materials.

## Conclusion
Encourage students to regularly update their study guides and adapt them based on ongoing learning and feedback.
