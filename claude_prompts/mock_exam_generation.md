# Mock Exam Generation Prompt Templates

## O Level Exam Generation Prompts

### Subject: Mathematics
- **Prompt:** Generate a comprehensive math exam for O Level including multiple choice, short answer, and problem-solving questions covering algebra, geometry, and statistics. Provide a marking scheme for each type of question.

### Subject: Science
- **Prompt:** Create an O Level science exam with sections on biology, chemistry, and physics. Include practical questions and theoretical questions, along with an answer key for easy grading.

### Subject: English Language
- **Prompt:** Write a mock O Level English exam focusing on reading comprehension, writing skills, and grammar. Include an essay topic and mark scheme for assessment.

## A Level Exam Generation Prompts

### Subject: Mathematics
- **Prompt:** Develop an A Level mathematics exam that includes pure math, statistics, and mechanics questions. Provide detailed solutions for every problem.

### Subject: Physics
- **Prompt:** Create a comprehensive A Level physics exam that tests on topics including mechanics, electricity, and waves. Include both theoretical questions and numerical problems with solutions.

### Subject: Chemistry
- **Prompt:** Generate a mock A Level chemistry exam covering organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry. Include questions that assess practical skills and theory understanding, along with a marking scheme.

### Subject: Literature
- **Prompt:** Write a mock A Level literature exam with questions that require analysis of texts and critical thinking, along with essay prompts for deeper exploration of themes and characters.
